# Video Background Setup

## How to Add Your Video

1. Place your video file in this directory (`frontend/public/videos/`)

2. Rename your video file to `background.mp4` or `background.webm`

3. The wizard page will automatically use it as the background

## Recommended Video Specs

- **Format**: MP4 (H.264) or WebM
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (Full HD) or 1280x720 (HD)
- **Duration**: 10-30 seconds (will loop)
- **File Size**: Keep under 10MB for fast loading
- **Content**: Travel scenes, landscapes, cityscapes, airports, etc.

## Current Settings

The video is displayed with the following effects:
- **Opacity**: 40% (to not overpower the form)
- **Brightness**: 50% (darkened for contrast)
- **Contrast**: 125% (slightly enhanced)
- **Saturation**: 110% (slightly boosted colors)
- **Gradient Overlay**: Dark gradient from black/80% to black/70%
- **Vignette**: Radial gradient for focus on center

## Fallback

If your local video is not found, the page will use a fallback video from:
https://cdn.pixabay.com/video/2021/08/04/83896-582967156_large.mp4
